Hi, I'm Brad, a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Chicago, IL, with my small growing family. My journey into tech started with ten years in education as a middle and high school math teacher. As I started teaching computer science classes, I became fascinated with web development and programming. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
